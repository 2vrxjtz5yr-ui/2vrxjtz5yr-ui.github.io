Cuba Libre / Rum and Coke with Lime

Serves 1 | Prep Time: 5 minutes

The Cuba libre ("free Cuba") is the legendary Cuban cocktail similar to rum and Coke but even tastier because of the fresh lime. Cuban exiles refer to this drink as a mentirita, or "little lie," because a free Cuba does not exist. No matter what you call it, this is more than a cocktail—it's a cry for freedom.

Ingredient Tip: White rum is the most popular ingredient for this drink, but you can use an aged or dark rum to turn this into a Cubata.

Ingredients

  • Ice cubes, for chilling
  • 1½ ounces white rum
  • 4 to 6 ounces Coca-Cola
  • 1 lime, halved, one half juiced, the other half sliced

Instructions

  1. Fill a tall glass two-thirds full of ice.
  2. Pour in the rum, Coke, and lime juice and stir to combine.
  3. Garnish with 1 or 2 lime slices and serve immediately.

Source: Recipe from The Essential Cuban Cookbook
