Batido de Mamey / Mamey Milk Shake

Prep Time: 10 minutes | 1 serving

With its bright salmon-color flesh, mamey produces a beautiful, creamy milk shake. If you cannot find mamey in your area, have no fear; the frozen pulp is widely available in most supermarkets. My neighbor (and one of my best friends, known as Tia Ofe to my children) adds a tablespoon of cream cheese to her milk shake for extra creaminess.

Variation Tip: This milk shake is delicious as is but can easily be made into a healthy smoothie. Swap almond milk or coconut milk for the whole milk and add a heaping teaspoon of chia seeds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cubed or frozen ripe mamey
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¼ cup sweetened condensed milk, plus more as needed
  • ½ cup crushed ice

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the mamey, whole milk, condensed milk, and ice. Blend until smooth. Add more sweetened condensed milk, to taste, before serving.

Source: Recipe from The Essential Cuban Cookbook
