Pasta de Bocadito / Ham Spread Sliders

Prep Time: 10 minutes, plus 2 hours to chill (optional) | Serves 4 to 6

It's just not a Cuban party without pasta de bocadito. These fluffy bite-size sweet roll sandwiches are filled with a creamy ham spread. Super easy to make and super delicious, these sliders are the equivalent of chips and dip. Make plenty, because once you try them, one won't be enough.

One Pot, Quick

Technique Tip: These sliders can be enjoyed immediately after they are made, but refrigerating them for at least two hours improves their texture and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup finely diced ham
  • ¼ cup sweet pickle relish, drained
  • ½ cup roasted red peppers, drained and finely diced
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 12 Hawaiian mini slider sweet rolls

Instructions

  1. In a food processor or a high-powered blender, combine the cream cheese, mayonnaise, ham, relish, red peppers, salt, and pepper. Pulse until the mixture is creamy and smooth.
  2. Spread the cream cheese mixture evenly on each roll and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Source: Recipe from The Essential Cuban Cookbook
